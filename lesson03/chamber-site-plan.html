Site Name

The site name I chose for this website is San Francisco Chamber of Commerce as it represents the city of San Francisco.

Site Purpose

The San Francisco Chamber of Commerce helps support and promote local activities and companies by sharing insights and thoughts to the community of San Francisco. It's purpose is to also help the city by listening and solving the problems the citizens of the community propose.
